[1] Surgical EQ (Fruity Parametric EQ 2)
    - Goal: Cut mud and subsonic rumble.

[2] Glue Compressor (Fruity Limiter / Bus Comp)
    - Goal: Cohesion.
    - Setting: Ratio 2:1, Attack 20-30ms, Release 50-100ms.
    - Reduction: 1-2dB max.

[3] Broad Tonal EQ (TDR Nova / Parametric EQ 2)
    - Goal: Final polish.
    - Setting: Subtle High-Shelf boost (+0.5dB to +1dB) above 10kHz.

[4] Saturation (Optional)
    - Goal: Warmth/Character.
    - Setting: Gentle drive to taste.

[5] Final Limiter (Limiter №6 / Fruity Limiter)
    - Goal: Loudness.
    - Setting: Ceiling @ -0.1dB. Increase gain to target levels.

[6] Metering (Voxengo SPAN)
    - Goal: Visual analysis.
    - Setting: Monitor frequency balance (0-200Hz focus).
